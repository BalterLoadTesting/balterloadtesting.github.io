Weekly Report

For business leaders, having quick access to high-level details is critical for keeping an eye on business metrics without being bogged down in details.

AutoBalter can be configured to provide weekly reports which contain important tracking metrics and highlights anomalous behavior. These can be surfaced in the Dashboard or sent via weekly Email updates.
